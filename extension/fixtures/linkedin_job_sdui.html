Senior AI Engineer

Northstar AI
San Francisco, CA · Reposted 3 days ago · 41 applicants

Description

Northstar AI builds frontier models that help people reason about hard problems. We are looking for a Senior AI Engineer to own model training infrastructure end to end.

About the AI Division

The AI Division is a small, senior team shipping research into production. You will work across data, training, and evaluation with direct ownership of outcomes.

About the Role

You will design distributed training pipelines, profile and optimize GPU utilization, and partner with researchers to take experiments from notebook to scaled run.

Responsibilities

  • Build and operate large-scale training infrastructure.
  • Improve throughput and reliability of training runs.
  • Mentor engineers and raise the team's technical bar.

Requirements

  • 5+ years building production ML systems.
  • Strong experience with PyTorch or TensorFlow.
  • Comfort with distributed systems and GPUs.

Qualifications

  • Degree in CS or equivalent practical experience.
  • Track record of shipping ML to production.
